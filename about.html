About Us

We are the World's Happiest Cafe

Founded by longtime friends Leejungseok Adams, a talented chef and Choi Sooyeonjun, an experienced roaster who pursued a career on both coasts, Happy Cafe offers a selection of custom, house roasted blends and seasonal single-origin beans that have been carefully sourced and specially roasted to provide the best drinking experience possible.
Happy Cafe is committed to producing only the freshest, highest quality coffee and cultivating strong relationships with our wholesale partners. We care deeply about what we do, we are proud to be partnered with some of the finest cafes, restaurants and shops throughout the city and beyond.
